NATURE

Blessed With All Four Seasons

As we all know,there are not so many countries in the world blessed with all four season. But in PAKISTAN we have four different and beautiful seasons.

Spring

As spring breathes new life into the world around us.Springtime is the land awakening.The march winds are the morning yawn.

Summer

Many sayings about spring and summer aren't just about the seasons.They have lots of other meanings and can be used in everyday life!

Fall(Autumn)

Notice that Autumn is more the season of soul than the nature.I am so glad I live in the world where ther are Octobers.

Winter

To appreciate the beauty of snow flake it is necessary to stand out in the cold. Winter is the time for comfort,for good food and warmth,for the touch of a friendly hand and for a talk beside the fire.
